Pocket Portraits Photography Series

A series of photographs featuring a set of objects obtained from individuals in an interview-like fashion. Each photograph, done within a whitebox, features whatever items the person may have had within their pockets, as a way to characterise and create a portrait of the person, in an unconventional way.
