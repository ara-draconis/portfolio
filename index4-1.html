North American Birding Field Guide

A design focused birding field guide for a wide audience, including novice and experienced birders. The process included many long stages of gathering information and imagery, as well as compressing it and sorting it into actual usable content for the book. The sides of the pages are color coded to allow for easier navigation, and the final design is intended to be spiral bound for quick page turning for when in the field. The cover sports an American Goldfinch, one of the most recognizable songbirds in a fun, illustrative style.
