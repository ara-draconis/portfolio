Creature Designs

A set of 3 creature designs. Each is created while keeping in mind how they may act if they were to actually exist. Designs are done with this semi-realism in mind, and making sure to not overaccentuate any fantastical elements. Studies of different animal parts are included to showcase the design/illustration process.
