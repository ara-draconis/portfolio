Ara Draconis

Hello! My name is Ara~

My main work consists usually of digital work, and I have about 5 years of graphic design experience. I enjoy and specialize in color and practice creating unique and cohesive color palettes that can be pleasing to any and all viewers.

My main area of focus is on iconography and logo design, but I also work in many other areas of design, such as typography, image design, motion design, publication design, and web design. I make sure to practice in these fields individually, as well as opportunistically using them in tandem with each other to elevate any of my projects that I feel could need it.

I’ve also worked extensively with illustration, photography, and animation. While I do focus on graphic design, I’ve has also branched out to learn many other art and design forms, such as 2d animation, 3d modeling, digital and physical drawing/painting, and even writing. Overall, I’ve worked with many softwares, artistic styles, mediums, and art forms. I use all of these learned and practiced skills together to create designs with great originality and cleanliness.
